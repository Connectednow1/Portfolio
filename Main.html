About Me

5 years of building
systems that work

Hey, I'm Alex, a Roblox scripter with 5 years of hands-on experience. I specialise in building solid & well-structured game systems using object oriented, modular architecture (though I can adapt to other structures). My code is clean, readable, and written with scalability in mind. If the project begs it, I WILL add detailed comments to the code.

I have contributed to a variety of projects; tycoons, simulators, RPGs, building games, survival games, etc. I can easily adapt to your project, since I have worked with several types of clients. If you don't prefer a modular architecture, I can do that as well.

I work fast. I communicate consistently. I'm familiar with ProfileService and other community modules, and I can also work with Rojo if that's what your workflow requires, though for my own work I don't necessarily find it to align with my ideal flow.

I do prefer COMMISSIONS over full games, because I typically don't like to spend too much time on one project. Hence, I'm not necessarily open for long-term work, but if you really want to you can review my prices and make an offer.

Capabilities

What I Can Build

A breakdown of what I'm experienced with, and where I have less mileage. The weaknesses list doesn't mean I'll refuse the work, it just means I'll move slightly slower on those.

✅

Strong At

Systems I am experienced with

  • Scripting UI: ScreenGui, animated menus, custom components
  • Basic AntiCheats: server-side validation, exploit detection
  • Combat Systems: hitboxes, combos, server-authoritative
  • Projectiles: physics-based, raycasting, custom trajectories
  • Movement Systems: standard and moderately custom locomotion
  • Placement Systems: moderately complex object placement
  • Tycoons: full loops, upgrade paths, conveyor logic
  • Simulator Mechanics: collection, stat loops, world scaling
  • Abilities: cooldowns, stacking, visual effect integration
  • Incrementals: idle economy and progression logic
  • Pet Systems: following AI, rarity buffs, storage vaults
  • Camera Systems: cutscenes, perspectives, lerp rigs
⚠️

Less Experienced With

Can still do these — just takes a bit longer

  • Vehicle Systems: drivetrains, setting up chassis, suspension physics, handling tuning
  • Complex Movement: polished flying mechanics, realistic locomotion feel, advanced character controllers
Important: I won't refuse these tasks — I just have less experience here, which means more iteration time. I'll always be upfront if something is pushing the edges of what I know well.

Transparency

AI Usage

A clear, honest breakdown of when I would and wouldn't use AI tools in development.

🚫

When I Would NOT Use AI

  • The client does not approve of AI usage and wants completely raw, manual input.
  • Most cases - if I deem the work to be too unscalable/requiring manual changes.
✅

When I Would Use AI

  • The client approves of some AI usage as long as it's not blind.
  • Never prompting it to create the full finished product - at the very least there will ALWAYS be manual review..

By default, everything I ship is pretty much all by me. If you want me to use AI assistance for any reason including speed, cost reduction, or anything else, then that's a conversation we can have before the project starts. You'll always know exactly what you're getting.
